IntersectElement



This class is a very close cousin of SurfaceElement. It describes anything one needs to know about the intersection between a photon path and a surface element. It is "more" than just a SurfaceElement since we may cross in or out, the Fresnel reflection depends on photon polarization, etc... It uses the same numbering scheme as surface elements.
